Bank Islam is a well-known Islamic banking institution based in Malaysia with over 10,000 branches and 6.2 million customers as of 2021. With the aim of providing financial services to help the needy, Bank Islam recently announced a new partnership with KOTA BHARU, a sub-district municipality located in Indonesia's West Kalimantan province. The agreement will facilitate Islamic financing and banking operations for the local Muslim community, including Islamic mortgages, credit, and investment products. This move is part of Bank Islam's efforts to expand its reach and offer more services to customers in Southeast Asia.
