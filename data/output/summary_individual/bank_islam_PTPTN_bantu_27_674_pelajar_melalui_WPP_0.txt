The article summarizes the bank's age-related assistance program, PBIM (Pembantu Bank Islam Malaysia), which offers financial products and services to individuals in their later years, with a specific focus on older adults. The articles highlight how these financial products are tailored to meet the unique needs of this population group, including providing guidance and support during the application process, offering affordable and flexible payment options, and ensuring accessibility through digital channels. Additionally, the article provides examples of PBIM's services in action, including helping seniors apply for and receive their Bersih cards, providing assistance with mortgage and insurance applications, and assisting with estate planning and financial management. The article also emphasizes that age-related assistance programs are a necessary aspect of a comprehensive approach to financial inclusion, and that the bank is committed to meeting the needs of this population group in a responsible and sustainable way.
