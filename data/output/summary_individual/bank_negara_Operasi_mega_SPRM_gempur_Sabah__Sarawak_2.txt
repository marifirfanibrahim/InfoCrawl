The article reports on Operation Mega by the Malaysian Anti-Corruption Commission (MACC) from 2nd to 8th September, which uncovered a corruption network involving the illegal sale of diesel and other assets valued at RM247 million over a period of 10 years in Sabah and Sarawak. The operation involved specialized teams from multiple agencies, including Bank Negara Malaysia (BNM). The investigation uncovered bank accounts, luxury vehicles, gold, land, boats, diesel, cryptocurrency, and cash amounts in foreign currencies totaling millions of ringgit. The operation is ongoing and more individuals are expected to be questioned and recorded statements taken.
