Bank Islam Malaysia Berhad (ISMB) recently announced the appointment of Nik Mohd Hasyudeen Yusoff as an Ahli Pengerusi LEMAGBAGA PENGARAH PERDANA (LMP), a position that has been vacant since January 15, 2025. This is based on the specifications stated in a recent announcement by the Bank, where it states that the postholder must have extensive experience and knowledge of Islamic banking and financial services, as well as possess excellent leadership skills. Additionally, the announcement notes that the LMP has a wide range of responsibilities, including management of operations, supervisory functions, and strategic planning for the Bank's Islamic banking segment. Furthermore, the announcement emphasizes that this position is subject to the approval of the Central Bank of Malaysia (CBM), with the appointment effective from January 15, 2025.
